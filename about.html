Our Journey

How It All Started

Founded in 2020 by a team of agronomists and tech engineers, HydroFeed emerged from a shared frustration: traditional livestock feed production was unsustainable, water-intensive, and weather-dependent.

We asked ourselves: What if farmers could grow fresh, nutrient-rich fodder anywhere, anytime, using minimal resources? That question became our mission.

Today, HydroFeed serves over 500 farms across 4 continents, delivering smart hydroponic solutions that are changing the way farmers feed their livestock.
