Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

Setup fees vary by plan. Starter plans have no setup fee. Grower and Professional plans have a one-time $499 setup fee. Enterprise is custom.

What's included in the dashboard?

All plans include access to track recurring feed deliveries, tray inventory, feeding schedules, and basic nutritional data. Higher tiers add advanced analytics and API access.

Can I try HydroFeed before committing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of our dashboard and a free consultation for system design.

Not sure which plan is right for you?

Talk to our team — we'll help you find the perfect solution for your farm.
